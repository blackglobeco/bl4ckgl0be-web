02 — CAPABILITIES

Intelligence. Precision. Reach.

Black Globe deploys a layered suite of cyber intelligence capabilities — each engineered for sovereign-grade performance and lawful, accountable use.

SIGINT & Network Surveillance

Passive and active signals collection across encrypted and cleartext communications infrastructure. Real-time analysis of network traffic at scale.

Endpoint Intelligence

Covert collection from targeted devices — mobile and desktop. Extraction of communications, location data, and behavioral patterns from hardened endpoints.

Threat Actor Attribution

Multi-vector analysis combining network forensics, behavioral fingerprinting, and OSINT to attribute cyber operations to state or non-state actors with high confidence.

Critical Infrastructure Protection

Persistent monitoring and hardening of national critical infrastructure — energy grids, financial systems, and communications backbone — against adversarial intrusion.

Geospatial & Location Intelligence

Real-time and historical location tracking fused with signals and open-source data to build comprehensive movement profiles of persons of interest.

Predictive Threat Analytics

Machine-learning models trained on classified datasets to forecast threat actor behavior, anticipate attack vectors, and generate actionable intelligence before incidents occur.
